Get Started

Interactively plan your set with SMART DJ assistance.

SMART DJ is a software created to help you plan your Dj sets based on your music library and mixing rules.

Either to plan which tracks you will play and in which order, or to record a DJ set and upload it to the internet, Smart DJ makes the process more efficient while allowing you to explore all mixing possibilities.

Visualize your music library in a new way

The library network view allows you to inspect your music library in a new way, allowing you to identify bottlenecks and possible options to enrich your music collection in a smart way.

Get insights on your set dynamics

Visualise your resulting DJ Set dynamics based on Key, BPM, energy, and many more metrics to come.
